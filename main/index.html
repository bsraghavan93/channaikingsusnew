Our Story

A Hidden Gem
at the Wharf

Nestled just steps from Pier 39 and Fisherman's Wharf, Chennai Kings has been bringing the bold, aromatic soul of Tamil Nadu to San Francisco since 2014. Our chefs craft every dish with authentic spice blends and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

From the crispy golden dosas to the richly spiced Chettinad curries and the fragrant biryanis — every plate is a journey to Chennai. The sambar and chutney are legendary, and our Kothu Parotta is a showstopper that keeps guests coming back.
